This a special bonus section.

As you may know the course includes a weekly contest where students participate by simply submitting a program they have built on their own. You can submit your own program in the Q&A section. Every week I select the winning app and I upload a video in this section. In the video I review the code of the app and explain what could be improved.

If you want to be part of the contest, below are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Build a Python program on your own or improve one of the existing programs covered in The Python Mega Course. 

Step 2: Save the source files on Github, Bitbucket, Dropbox, or on any other sharable cloud service. 

Step 3: Create a question in the Q&A section inside the course, and submit the link where your files can be downloaded from, and also write a few lines describing what the program does.

Then, just be on the lookout for an email from me by the end of the week where I will announce the winning app, and give you the link to the code review video where I review the code of the app.
